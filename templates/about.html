About us

Welcome to MakersBnB – your go-to platform for affordable and unique spaces. Founded in 2025 by six software engineers – Andrew, Will, Jack, Joseph, John and Luis – we set out to make finding and sharing spaces easier and more accessible for everyone. Whether you need a creative studio, a cozy retreat, or a productive workspace, MakersBnB connects you with the perfect spot at a price that works. We believe that great ideas and experiences start with the right space – and we’re here to help you find it.
